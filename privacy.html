Health Reboot Automator – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Health Reboot Automator (“we,” “us,” or “Service”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our Make.com integration to automate video posting and commenting on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

2. Information We Collect

  • OAuth Tokens: We store encrypted OAuth access and refresh tokens only to authenticate with each platform’s API on your behalf.
  • Content Metadata: We log video IDs, timestamps, captions generated, and comment IDs for operational and auditing purposes.
  • Affiliate Link Data: We store the affiliate URLs you provide in your Google Sheet catalog so we can insert them into comments.

We do not collect or store your passwords or any sensitive profile data beyond what’s required for API access.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To post videos and comments on your behalf via the platforms’ APIs.
  • Logging & Troubleshooting: To record which posts succeeded or failed, so we can retry or alert you in case of errors.
  • Analytics (Optional): If you enable logging to Google Sheets or Make Data Store, we may store performance metrics (views, clicks, conversions) purely for your dashboard—never for marketing or third‑party sale.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your data to unaffiliated third parties. We share OAuth tokens and minimal metadata only with TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube as necessary to deliver the Service. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect against fraud or security breaches.

5. Data Retention

  • OAuth Tokens: Retained until you revoke access or delete the Make.com scenario.
  • Logs & Metadata: Retained for up to 12 months by default, after which older records are purged.
  • Affiliate Catalog: Stored in your Google Sheet; we only read from it when your scenario runs.

6. Security Measures

All OAuth tokens and log data are encrypted at rest within Make.com’s infrastructure. We use HTTPS/TLS for all API calls to ensure data in transit is secure. We follow least‑privilege principles: only the minimum scopes required (`video.upload`, `video.publish`, `comment.publish`) are requested.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • Revoke Access: You can disconnect the Service at any time by removing the Make.com connection or revoking OAuth permissions in your platform account settings.
  • Data Deletion: Email us at the address below to request deletion of any stored logs or metadata before the 12‑month retention period.
  • Updates & Notifications: If we materially change this policy, we will post an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is intended for users aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
